Queris

A New Kind of QA

Queris is an open-source question and answer platform for programmers that places an emphasis on community-driven discourse and an openness to beginners.

It’s a place where simply asking a question doesn’t make you have to worry about being downvoted or having your questions closed. And everything is available to you right off the bat, to prioritize user engagement and community interaction.
